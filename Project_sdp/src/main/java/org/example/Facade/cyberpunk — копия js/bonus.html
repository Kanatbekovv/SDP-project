Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is an action/RPG video game developed and published by CD Projekt. The game is set in the year 2077 in Night City, a fictional North American city from the Cyberpunk universe.

About this game

  • Cyberpunk 2077 is an action role-playing game played from a first-person perspective as V, a mercenary whose voice, face, hairstyle, body type, modifications, background, and clothing are customizable. There are also five attributes (Body, Intelligence, Reflexes, Technical ability, and Cool) that can be customized to suit the player's gameplay style.
  • Cyberpunk 2077 was created according to the canons of the "old" cyberpunk of the 1980s.
  • Technology plays a crucial role in gameplay. Players can enhance V's abilities through cybernetic implants.
