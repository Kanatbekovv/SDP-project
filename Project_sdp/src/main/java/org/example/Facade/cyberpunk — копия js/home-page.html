CYBERPUNK 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is a computer game in the action/RPG genre, developed and published by the Polish studio CD Projekt. The game takes place in the year 2077 in Night City, a fictional North American city from the Cyberpunk universe.









Sales

The number of pre-orders for the game on all platforms reached 8 million copies; this allowed the costs of production and marketing to be recouped right from the start. According to CD Projekt RED, over 13 million copies of the game were sold across all platforms within the first 10 days of release—both physical and digital versions, taking into account returns.



Setting

The game takes place in 2077 in Night City, a fictional metropolis on the west coast of North America. According to the game's backstory.

Story

The game begins with the choice of one of three life paths for V. All three options converge on the fact that V starts a new life and gains new friends: the gangster Jackie Welles and the hacker T-Bug.

Development and Release

The development of Cyberpunk 2077 began in 2012. That same year, Mike Pondsmith started consulting with the developers.

Concept

Cyberpunk 2077 is based on Mike Pondsmith's tabletop game Cyberpunk 2020. Key elements are taken from the 2020 edition, but the events of the game take place in a later future, in 2077.

Soundtrack

The musical score for the game was created by three composers: Polish Marcin Przybyłowicz and P.T. Adamczyk, and British Paul Leonard-Morgan.

Content Critique

Reviewer Kelly Pledge from GameSpot rated Cyberpunk 2077 at 7 out of 10 points, noting the diversity and flexibility of character customization as positive aspects of the game.
